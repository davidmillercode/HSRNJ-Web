Jeffrey K. Miller, MD

Orthopedic Hand Surgeon

Board certified in orthopedic surgery and fellowship trained in hand surgery, Dr. Jeffrey K. Miller offers the highest level of personalized care to patients with disorders of the hand, wrist and elbow. His commitment to his patients is evident in the amount of time he devotes to them, listening to their symptoms and concerns, thoroughly explaining his diagnosis and discussing treatment options. “I see myself more as a hand doctor than a hand surgeon,” he says. “When patients come to me, I don’t immediately think of what operation I can do. Rather, I first look at nonsurgical treatment options. Therapy, an injection, casting, ergonomic adjustments or other nonoperative measures are often the best treatment. Surgery is sometimes an option, but frequently it’s not the treatment of choice.”

Specializing in complex fractures of the hand, wrist and forearm, as well as arthroscopic treatment of wrist disorders, Dr. Miller is proud to be considered one of the top hand surgeons in New Jersey. He has received numerous Castle Connolly Medical Ltd. Top Doctor and Best Doctors in America honors. Additionally, he has been named a New Jersey Monthly top doctor for the past three years. He credits his dedicated staff, who promptly answer all phone calls and uphold the practice’s personalized service philosophy, with helping him gain the trust of both patients and colleagues during his 27 years in practice.
