Hand Therapy & Rehabilitation

Hand therapy is an important aspect of treating the injured or post-surgical hand, wrist, or elbow, and can make a significant difference in the recovery process.

Hand therapy is available next to our Morristown and Union offices. We can also coordinate therapy at other locations if necessary.

With hand therapy, our goal is to restore the hand, wrist, or elbow to the best possible condition, allowing the patient to return to work, sports, and activities of daily living as soon as possible.

In addition, we offer evaluation and recommendations for workstation and job site design, to help create ergonomic workspaces that minimize or prevent disorders of the hand, wrist, and elbow.
